Rules
Click your hex to select it. Then click an adjacent hex to send half your troops. Blue ocean tiles are impassable. Captures happen when attackers exceed defenders. Every faction gets growth at the start of its turn (+1 each hex, +2 on capital). Defeat a rival leader by taking their capital to capture their queen. Captured queens have morale, assignments, and unique units with fight/serve modes.
